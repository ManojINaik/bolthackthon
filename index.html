About the Hackathon

Welcome to The World's Largest Hackathon, hosted by bolt.new and led by @gregisenberg! With an unprecedented $1M+ prize pool, we're aiming to set a Guinness World Record by bringing together over 100,000 builders from across the globe in a groundbreaking virtual event.

Mark your calendars for May 2025! This isn't just a competition - it's a launchpad for the next generation of tech visionaries. The virtual format ensures anyone can participate from anywhere, breaking down geographical barriers to innovation. Expect digital collaboration tools, virtual team formation opportunities, and brainstorming sessions to fuel your creativity.

Whether you're a seasoned developer, a creative designer, or an ambitious entrepreneur, this is your chance to build something revolutionary. Form teams of 2-5 members or participate solo - entry is completely free. Top winners will receive life-changing prizes, mentorship from industry titans, and introductions to eager investors ready to take your project to the next level.
